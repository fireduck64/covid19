Customize

You can customize a chart using any US states and counties. Simply modify one of the above links with any additional locations in the URL. Locations are separated by '/' and counties are specified as State,County. cases.html can be replaced with death.html to chart covid-19 deaths instead.

Options

  • include_delta - adds a line showing the rate of change for each location
